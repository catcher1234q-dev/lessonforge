# Payment Provider Readiness

This note is for internal use when LessonForgeHub applies to a marketplace-friendly payment provider.

## Approved Business Description

LessonForgeHub operates a digital marketplace for teacher resources.

Educators upload original classroom materials, including worksheets, practice packs, task cards, warm-ups, and unit resources.

Customers purchase digital downloads through the platform and access purchased files in their buyer library.

## How The Business Makes Money

LessonForgeHub earns revenue through marketplace transaction splits and seller subscription plans.

Sellers keep a plan-based share of each sale, and the platform keeps the remaining marketplace share.

## How Funds Flow

Customers purchase digital products through LessonForgeHub.

Payments are processed through a payment provider.

LessonForgeHub records the transaction, controls product access, and routes seller payouts through the platform after transactions clear and platform rules are satisfied.

Payouts may be delayed or adjusted when a transaction is refunded, disputed, charged back, or flagged for policy review.

## Risk Controls

LessonForgeHub is a controlled marketplace, not an open file dump.

The platform enforces:

- listing review and moderation
- product reporting
- seller ownership responsibility
- copyright compliance expectations
- policy-based listing removal
- visible support contact
- a strict digital refund policy
- protected buyer library access after purchase

## Plain-Language Positioning

LessonForgeHub is a digital marketplace for teacher resources.

Educators upload original classroom materials.

Customers purchase digital downloads.

The platform facilitates payment processing and seller payouts.

The platform enforces content policies, product reporting, listing review, and a strict digital refund policy.
